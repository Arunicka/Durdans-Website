Compassion, Innovation and Excellence

Welcome to Durdans Hospital

Since our foundation in 1945, we have built a reputation for regional leadership in medical excellence and innovation, based on a simple philosophy: that improving the health of our community should be driven by passion as well as compassion. We offer a range of spacious modern rooms and are equipped with state of critical care units. At Durdans the best consultants, specialists and employees are dedicated to providing exceptional clinical outcomes and the utmost customer satisfaction.

Durdans Hospital in Sri Lanka offers a wide range of advanced healthcare services, making it a leading medical facility in the region. Key services include:

1. Cardiac Care: Durdans is renowned for its Heart Centre, offering cutting-edge procedures like Transcatheter Aortic Valve Implantation (TAVI), open-heart surgeries, and comprehensive cardiac diagnostics.

2. International Patient Care: The hospital provides seamless care for international patients, including assistance with travel, accommodation, and personalized treatment plans.

3. Advanced Diagnostics and Imaging: Equipped with state-of-the-art imaging technologies, Durdans offers precise diagnostics, including MRI, CT scans, and other advanced radiological services.

4. Surgical Services: The hospital provides a wide array of surgical treatments across various specialties, including general surgery, orthopedics, and minimally invasive procedures.

5. Emergency Services: Durdans offers 24/7 emergency care with a dedicated team of specialists ready to handle critical situations with efficiency and care.
